Welcome to San Diego!

This year's venue is the San Diego Marriott of Mission Valley! Located just 20 minutes from San Diego International Airport and under 15 minutes to the glorious San Diego Zoo, the Marriott of Mission Valley provides the perfect setting.

San Diego Marriott of Mission Valley is only steps away from the beautiful San Diego River Trail, which is great for your morning run or bike ride.

An Accessible Experience

We've worked with the staff at the San Diego Marriott to accommodate the diverse needs of our attendees.

Are we missing something? Let us know.

  • There is step-free access to all parts of the venue, from the parking lot to the presentation rooms.
  • Restrooms include an ADA stall with grab bars, but do not contain lowered sinks.
  • San Diego Marriott does not provide gender-neutral restrooms. Please use the restroom for the gender with which you identify.
  • Service animals are welcome, and there is a large park directly behind the hotel where they can be walked.
